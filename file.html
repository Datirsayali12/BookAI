Chapter 2: Goal Setting and Time Blocking

This chapter focuses on goal setting and time blocking as essential tools for effective time management. Setting SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound) goals provides clarity and direction. Time blocking involves allocating specific time slots for different tasks, ensuring focus and productivity. Overcoming procrastination is key to efficient time management. By understanding the root causes of procrastination and implementing strategies to combat it, you can enhance your time management skills.
